CASA DE VIDA

Arpora, Goa

Bookings Open!

CASA DE VIDA, A Spanish name which means The House of Life is our fourth project situated in Arpora, Goa. The project has an individual Villa and 42 apartments, each one designed beautifully, consisting of smart studio apt, 1 BHK, 2 BHK and 3 BHK. The project is spread over 2376 sqr mtr of land with 4 floors and basement parking.


CASA DE VIDA nestled pleasantly in the centre of North Goa, you'll be immersed in beautiful surroundings, where you can pick out salt plans, whitewashed churches and rice paddies backed by a forest of palm trees. Arpora is also a great base to explore the surrounding beach resorts of Baga and Calangute, giving you the luxury to be able to head back to your rural retreat at the end of a busy day.


CASA DE VIDA, is not just a home garnished with a sunny Goan advantage, but a "Where in Goa" advantage too.
